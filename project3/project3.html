The Queen Feeding Fruits to a Peacock

by Raja Ravi Varma

The painting of The Queen Feeding Fruits to a Peacock was painted in 1876 by an artist Raja Ravi Varma. The work had won praise for Ravi Varma when it was presented at the Madras Fine Arts Exhibition of 1876.

Date: 1876
Medium: Oil on Canvas
Dimensions: 100 x 70 cm
Home: Gallery of Modern Art, Delhi;
